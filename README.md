# Vaultix

Vaultix is an Android app that lets you lock other apps on your phone for a set amount of time. Pick the apps you want to block, choose how long, and hit lock. Good for staying focused or keeping certain apps out of reach.

There are two lock modes:
- **Soft lock** — you can unlock early from the app if you need to
- **Hard lock** — stays locked until the timer runs out or you restart the phone

The app also supports a 4-digit PIN to prevent accidental unlocks, and keeps a history of past lock sessions.

Built with Flutter + Kotlin.
